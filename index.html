The Taxonomies of Visual Cognition

Building a data visualizaton requires consideration of audience perception through a variety of lenses. Visual cognition involves the intellectual functions involved in perceiving and processing information through visual modality. It is an important pillar of information design as it provides us with a framework for how an audience considers and identifies patterns within quantitative and qualitative data.

The study of visual cognition has produced various taxonomies that provide a framework for design work to appeal to the visual system and facilitate easier and more accurate extraction of information. While these taxonomies are not strict laws, but rather considerations in building a data visualization, the interplay of these various guidelines provide guidelines for mapping information to visual form.
